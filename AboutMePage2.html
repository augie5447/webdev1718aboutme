All About Soccer

    Football or soccer, is a team sport played between two teams of eleven players with a ball. It is played by 250 million players in over 200 countries and dependencies, making it the world's most popular sport. The game is played on a rectangular field with a goal at each end. The object of the game is to score by getting the ball into the opposing goal. The team with the most points after two 45 minute halfs is the winner unless extra time is needed whihc adds another 30 minutes, and finally if a winner is not decided it is then decided by a penalty shootout. According to FIFA, the Chinese competitive game cuju is the earliest form of football for which there is evidence. Cuju players could use any part of the body apart from hands and the intent was kicking a ball through an opening into a net.
