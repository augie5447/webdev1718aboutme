My Favorite Novel

The Great Gatsby is the story of eccentric millionaire Jay Gatsby as told by Nick Carraway, a Midwesterner who lives on Long Island but works in Manhattan. Teen Titans TV Show

Teen Titans centers around the five main members of the superhero team: Robin, the intelligent, capable leader of the Teen Titans; Starfire, a quirky, curious alien princess from the planet Tamaran; Cyborg, a half-human/half-robot who is known for his strength and technological prowess; Raven, who draws upon dark energy and psionic abilities; and Beast Boy, a ditzy, good-natured joker who can transform into various animals.

Gravity Falls TV Show

For their summer vacation, 12-year-old Dipper Pines and his twin sister Mabel are dropped off from their home in Piedmont, California to the fictitious town of Gravity Falls, Roadkill County, Oregon to spend the summer with their Great Uncle Stan Pines.

Star vs. The Forces Of Evil TV Show

Star Butterfly is a magical princess from the dimension of Mewni, and the heir to the royal throne of the Butterfly Kingdom. She befriends Marco Diaz and lives with his family while attending Echo Creek Academy. Star and Marco must deal with everyday school life while protecting Star's wand from falling into the hands of Ludo, a villain from Mewni who commands a group of monsters.
