Yugioh

All About Yugioh

    Yu-Gi-Oh is a Japanese manga series about gaming written and illustrated by Kazuki Takahashi. The first anime production was Yugioh season 0 in April of 1998, then another anime was created called Yugioh! Duel Monsters, which began in 2000. The popularity sparked a franchise that includes multiple spin-off manga and anime series, a trading card game, and numerous video games. Most of the franchises involve the trading card game known as Duel Monsters, where each player uses cards to "duel". This forms the basis for the real life Yu-Gi-Oh! Trading Card Game. With the anime's success various movies have also adapted Yugioh such as the most recent movie Yugioh!: The Dark Side Of The Dimesions. The game has continued to evolve throughout the years with each new spin-off bringing hundreds of new cards and new game mechanics to add to the trading card game. In total there are 5 spin-offs of the original Yugioh with each bringing new things to the table. The first spin-off is Yugioh! GX, this new series brought fusions monsters to the game giving players new strategies. The next series was Yugioh! 5D's, this series brought tuner and synchro monsters giving players hundreds of new cards to play with. The third spin-off was Yugioh! Zexal, this series brought Xyz monsters which gave players new ways to summon monsters. The next series was Yugioh! Arc-V, this series brought pendulum monsters giving players the chance to use all previous summoning methods easily. And the current spin-off is Yugioh! Vrains adding Link monsters, which changed where players placed their cards during the game. Yugioh is constantly evolving with new card packs being released every month and with new ways to play the card game is still alive.
